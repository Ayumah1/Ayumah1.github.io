Superstore Sales Dashboard
Objectives

Analyzed Superstore sales data to uncover trends, optimize product strategies, and support decision-making.

Tools Used
  • Power BI: For interactive visualizations and slicers.
  • Excel: For initial data cleaning and validation.
Key Metrics & Insights
  • Revenue: $2.3M across 9994K units sold.
  • Profit Margin: 12%, with top product (Canon imageCLASS) contributing to the most profits.
  • Seasonal Trends: Sales peak in 2014.
  • Regional Analysis: The West and East regions lead in sales volume.
Impact

Enabled stakeholders to prioritize high-margin products and target key regions, improving profitability. Demonstrates skills in data visualization, DAX, and actionable insights.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Excel's limitation of visual design and interactivity led me to explore Power BI where I added drill-downs and a sleeker look.
Solution: Applied imputation techniques in Excel to ensure data integrity.
